But most prominent among all work presented will be the project work with the KICK (**K**inematically **I**nterchangeable **C**ontrol **K**it) Robot, a novel open-source platform under ongoing development by yours truly. We're talking about full-stack hardware and software development, from from 3D CAD work and motor selection to ROS architecture.

## A Brief Word on KICK Robotics
Picture this: a 3D-printable kit that allows you to take a leftover shoebox, raspberry pi, and some other electronics, and get a mobile robot out of it.

With me so far? 

Did you picture your mobile robot as having wheels or legs? 

For the KICK robot I'm developing, it won't matter. You will be able to freely switch between the two, provided you have the right modules printed out. You'll even be able to change where they are attached to the box/robot body, thanks to the rail system this kit will employ.
